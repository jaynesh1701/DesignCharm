Frequently asked questions

What kinds of businesses do you work with?
We work worldwide with the following types of companies: Jewelry/Diamond retail and manufacturing, Luxury, fashion and other.
Why not a full-time designer?
A good full-time senior-level designer costs at least $70,000 annually, plus employee benefits and availability issues. The monthly plan lets you pause and resume as needed, saving costs by paying only for available work.
How does design request work / How do you work?
We use Trello for client design requests. Clients add requests along with the project information we need for the design (detailed design briefs are preferred). We work on the request, submit it to you for feedback, and make revisions until you are satisfied. We are very flexible and can accommodate your preferred method. Don't worry if you are unfamiliar with Trello, we'll be happy to guide you through how it works.
How fast will I receive my designs?
We try to always ship as quickly as possible. Typically, simple design requests are completed within 1-2 days, while complex tasks may take 3-4 days or more, depending on their level of complexity."
Who are the designers?
Designcharm is run by two experienced senior designers, Harshal and Jaynesh. You will be working directly with the founders.
What programs do you design in?
We mostly use Figma, Photoshop and illustrator. Blender for 3d. We are not bound to any specific programs, we can adapt tools based on required design work.
Is there a limit to how many requests I can have? Is it truly unlimited designs
Yes, you can add an unlimited number of design requests to the Trello board. We will work on them one at a time, with other tasks waiting in queue.
Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?
We offer a 7-day free trial to ensure a good fit before starting work. Due to the high-quality of our designs, no refunds will be given. Our significant time investment in the designs makes refunds unfeasible
